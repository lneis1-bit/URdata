Every time you scroll, search, or click, someone is watching. Something is cashing in on your every digital move. This site breaks down how tech corporations collect your personal data, why it's a problem, and what you can do about it.

The Problem

Most people don't realize the sheer scale of data being collected about them every day. Your location, habits, relationships, and beliefs are all being harvested, stored, and sold without your meaningful consent.

This is a serious ethical issue that violates people's privacy, and it only continues to worsen.
